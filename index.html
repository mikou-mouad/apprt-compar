*2-year rent+furniture+energy = rent × 24, plus monthly charges (charges locatives — copro/maintenance charges billed on top of rent every month, not to be confused with the one-time agency fee) × 24, plus a one-time ~€5,000 furniture estimate for unfurnished units, plus the one-time agency fee (frais d'agence, "charge agence" — charged by some agencies on entry, not others; enter 0 if none), plus (elec €/mo + gas €/mo) × 24. Electricity always applies regardless of heating type (lighting, appliances, plugs are always billed to the tenant) — pick an Elec class for every apartment. Gas only applies when heating is "Individual · Gas heating" — if heating is collective or individual-electric, gas is "n/a" (no separate gas bill; collective heating cost is already bundled into the rent shown as charges). Each cost = DPE class midpoint (kWh/m²/year — A≈50, B≈90, C≈150, D≈215, E≈290, F≈375, G≈450) × apartment surface × your price per kWh, divided by 12.
